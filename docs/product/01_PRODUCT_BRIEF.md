# Product Brief — Community First

## Problem
DIY travel planning requires many sources (reviews, weather, safety, logistics, budget), creating friction and anxiety.

## Target
Families, couples, sea-lovers, travelers who prefer authentic destinations over mass tourism.

## MVP input form
- Travel dates or month
- Duration
- Budget range (total)
- Departure airports (optional)
- Pace: slow/medium/intense
- Priorities: sea, snorkeling, nature, culture, food, kids-friendly, relax
- Constraints: no_car, low_crowd, high_safety, no_generic_resort

## MVP output (2–4 proposals)
- Why it fits (bullets)
- Day-by-day plan
- POIs with evidence links + map links
- Budget breakdown with min/max ranges + assumptions
- Seasonality/weather notes + risks/checks
- Follow-up questions when info missing

## Community KPIs
Form completion, share/copy rate, time on page, feedback ratio.
